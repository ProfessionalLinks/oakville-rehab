What to Expect During Treatment

Understanding what to expect during treatment can ease anxieties and help individuals prepare for the rehab process. Typically, the treatment journey includes initial assessments, detoxification if necessary, individual and group therapy, and aftercare planning. Each stage is designed to support the patient through their recovery journey.

Initial Assessment and Detoxification

The initial assessment involves evaluating the patient's physical and psychological state. Detoxification, if required, is managed under medical supervision to ensure safety and comfort. This stage prepares individuals for more intensive therapeutic interventions.

Therapy and Counseling Sessions

Therapy and counseling are central to the treatment process. These sessions aim to address the psychological aspects of addiction and provide coping strategies. Patients participate in individual therapy to explore personal issues and group therapy to connect with others facing similar challenges.
